How does it work?

Our Feature Matrix packages contain engineered features (indicators, oscillators, statistical measures) derived from crypto price and volume series. They are structured as TSV/CSV files grouped by symbol and timeframe. Each row corresponds to a timestamp (UTC) with dozens of feature columns.

To use:

  1. After purchase, download the ZIP archive via your chosen platform (Gumroad).
  2. Verify the SHA‑256 hash using your local tool (e.g. sha256sum file.zip) to ensure integrity.
  3. Extract the archive and load the TSV/CSV files into your preferred environment (Python, R, etc.).
  4. For neural networks, convert to tensors or feed directly into Geneva’s EV/ONNX models.
